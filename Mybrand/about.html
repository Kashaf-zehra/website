About Us

With over 03 years of experience, TrendyThreads continues its legacy of providing superior fabric,value for money and trendsetting apparel to its It all began in 2017 when the textile industry had not even flourished to its complete potential, that TrendyThreads initiated ready-made winter wear, particularly knits for men, women and children. From then on, TrendyThreads became a prestigious brand and won respect as the master of detail in the realm of ready-made garments manufacturing.

TrendyThreads stands as an enormous textile giant today. However, looking at the market potential amongst clothing brands for women, TrendyThreads furthermore ventured into creating its exclusive women’s clothing in 2017 which has now become a one-stop clothing solution for the women of Pakistan . Thus we became to be known as TrendyThreads.

The umbrella brand is now clearly – Women’s Prêt, Unstitched, and Ready To Wear.
